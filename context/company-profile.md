## Product

NimbusFlow is a cloud-based project management platform built for remote-first engineering and product teams. It combines task tracking, sprint planning, real-time collaboration, and automated reporting in one unified workspace.

### Core Plans

| Plan | Price | Users | Storage | Key Features |
|------|-------|-------|---------|--------------|
| **Starter** | Free | Up to 5 | 5 GB | Basic tasks, 3 projects |
| **Growth** | $12/user/mo | Unlimited | 50 GB | Sprints, time tracking, integrations |
| **Business** | $29/user/mo | Unlimited | 500 GB | Custom workflows, SSO, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited | Dedicated CSM, SLA, on-prem option |
